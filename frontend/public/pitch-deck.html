The Solution

LaundroBot replaces the encoder,
the notebook, and the missed payments.

AI Chatbot (Tagalog & English)
Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash. Answers FAQs, explains pricing, guides customers to book — 24/7 in Tagalog, English, and Taglish.
Messenger-native Booking
Customer taps "Book Now" inside Messenger. A webform slides up in-chat. No app download, no account creation.
Kanban Order Board
All orders from Messenger, Instagram, and walk-in land in one drag-and-drop board. Nothing gets lost.
Payments + Auto-Reminders
Xendit payment links sent with every order. 4-stage reminders at 1hr, 3hr, 6hr, 23hr. Auto-cancel at 24 hours.
