The Solution

LaundroBot replaces the encoder,
the notebook, and the missed payments.

🤖

AI Chatbot (Tagalog & English)

Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash. Answers FAQs, explains pricing, and directs customers to book — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

📱

Messenger-native Booking

Customers tap "Book Now" inside Messenger. A webform slides up inside the chat. No app download, no new account, no friction.

📋

Kanban Order Board

All orders — Messenger, walk-in, Instagram — land in one drag-and-drop board. Staff see status at a glance. Nothing is lost.

💳

Online Payments + Auto-Reminders

Xendit payment links sent automatically. 4-stage reminders at 1hr, 3hr, 6hr, 23hr. Auto-cancel unpaid orders after 24 hours.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Direct outreach first.
Then community and referrals.

Direct Outreach

  • DM laundry shops on Facebook/Instagram directly
  • Offer free 30-day trial, assisted setup
