The Solution

LaundroBot replaces the encoder,
the notebook, and the missed payments.

🤖
AI Chatbot (Tagalog & English)
Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash. Answers FAQs, explains pricing, guides customers to book — 24/7. Speaks Tagalog, English, and Taglish naturally.
📱
Messenger-native Booking
Customer taps "Book Now" inside Messenger. A webform slides up inside the chat. No app download, no account creation, zero friction.
📋
Kanban Order Board
All orders from Messenger, Instagram, walk-in land in one drag-and-drop board. Staff see status instantly. Nothing is lost.
💳
Payments + Auto-Reminders
Xendit payment links auto-sent with every order. 4-stage reminders at 1hr, 3hr, 6hr, 23hr. Auto-cancel unpaid at 24 hours.
